## A) FINGERPRINT D CONCEPT

### A1. Main Icon (Primary)

```
Professional logo icon design for "Orisios" dental software brand. The letter "D" constructed entirely from fingerprint-style curved parallel lines, forming an elegant continuous pattern.

STRUCTURE:
- The outer boundary forms a perfect uppercase "D" shape with the straight vertical edge on the left side
- Inside the D: concentric curved lines following fingerprint whorl pattern, spacing between lines exactly 3-4px at 512px canvas
- HIDDEN ELEMENT: Within the fingerprint pattern, subtly integrated tooth silhouette - the tooth has rounded crown at top and two curved roots at bottom, the roots extend toward the bottom-right of the D
- The tooth shape is formed by strategic breaks and curves in the fingerprint lines, not a separate solid shape
- The fingerprint lines flow naturally around and through the tooth form, creating a "reveal" effect when you look closely

COLORS:
- Primary lines: Deep Teal gradient from #0D7377 (left side, darker) to #14919B (right side, lighter)
- Accent lines (forming the tooth interior): Coral #FF6B6B with 80% opacity, creating warmth in the center
- Some lines transition from teal to coral where they pass through the tooth area
- Subtle gradient glow on the right edge: Coral #FF6B6B at 15% opacity

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Canvas: 512x512px, scalable vector
- Line weight: 2.5px consistent throughout
- Line endings: Rounded caps
- Minimum spacing between lines: 3px
- Corner radius on D shape: 0px on inner vertical, smooth curve on outer arc
- The D should occupy 85% of the canvas with equal padding

STYLE:
- Minimalist, modern, geometric precision
- Clean vector aesthetic, no gradients on individual lines (flat color per line segment)
- Medical-tech feel: trustworthy yet innovative
- Silicon Valley startup meets Swiss design precision

AVOID:
- Literal tooth clipart
- Cartoonish style
- 3D effects or heavy shadows
- Overly complex fingerprint with too many lines (keep it elegant, 12-15 curved lines maximum)
- Generic biometric icon look

OUTPUT: Single icon on pure white #FFFFFF background, PNG with transparency, vector-ready

--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --style raw --s 250 --no text, words, letters beneath icon, realistic, photograph, 3d render
```

## B) HANDWRITTEN CALLIGRAPHY CONCEPT

### B1. Main Wordmark

```
Elegant hand-lettered logo for "orisios" in continuous single-line calligraphy style.

LETTERING:
- All lowercase "orisios"
- Single continuous line that never lifts
- Thin line weight: 1.5-2px consistent
- Modern calligraphy, Spencerian-influenced
- Slight rightward slant (~15-20 degrees)
- Bouncing baseline with subtle vertical rhythm

FLOURISHES:
- Entry stroke: 30-40px horizontal with slight upward curve before "d"
- Exit flourish on "y": 60-80px sweeping curve to right, hair-thin taper

LETTER DETAILS:
- Tall ascenders on d, t, f (40% above x-height)
- Graceful descender on y flowing into exit flourish
- Open, airy loops
- "t" crossbar integrated into flow (not separate stroke)

COLOR: Near Black #1A1A2E on white - timeless, elegant

MOOD: Intimate, caring, personal touch. Like a handwritten note.
```
