# Orisios Color History

> Evolution of color decisions for the Orisios brand

---

## Version 1.0 — January 2026

**Decision by:** Leo (Design Maestro)
**Date:** January 9, 2026

### Philosophy

Medical software traditionally drowns in boring blue and sterile white. Orisios should stand out while still inspiring trust. The combination of **Teal + Coral** is fresh, modern, but not frivolous.

### Primary Palette Chosen

| Color | HEX | Rationale |
|-------|-----|-----------|
| **Deep Teal** | `#0D7377` | Blue that grew up and moved to the sea. Combines reliability of blue (trust, professionalism) with refreshing energy of green (health, growth). Perfect for medical SaaS. |
| **Coral** | `#FF6B6B` | Adds warmth and humanity. In the world of cold medical interfaces, this is like a doctor's smile — immediately feels cozier. |

### Why NOT standard medical blue?

> "Medical software traditionally drowns in a sea of boring blue and sterile white. *Yawn.* Orisios should stand out while still inspiring trust."
> — Leo

### Supporting Colors

- **Mint** (`#45B7A0`) — Success, positive states
- **Light Teal** (`#14919B`) — Hover states, interaction feedback
- **Soft Teal** (`#E8F6F6`) — Subtle backgrounds

### Neutrals Philosophy

- **Near Black** (`#1A1A2E`) instead of pure `#000000` — pure black is too aggressive. Near Black has a slight blue undertone that harmonizes with the teal palette.
- All neutrals have consistent blue undertone for cohesion.

### Accessibility

All combinations verified against WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
