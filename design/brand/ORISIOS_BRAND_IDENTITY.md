## I. Brand Philosophy

### The Name: Orisios

**Etymology:** From Latin "os, oris" (mouth) -- the foundation of oral medicine, the gateway to health, the source of communication and expression.

**Why it works:**
- Direct connection to dental/oral medicine without being clinical
- Professional, almost pharmaceutical gravitas
- International pronunciation -- works in any language
- Tech-forward sound (the "-ios" suffix evokes iOS, suggesting digital sophistication)
- Unique in the dental SaaS space

**Brand Essence:**
> "Orisios -- where oral health meets intelligent technology"

### Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| **Professional** | We speak with authority, backed by medical expertise |
| **Intelligent** | OrisAI isn't just a feature -- it's our DNA |
| **Warm** | Technology that feels human, not cold |
| **Modern** | We're the future of dental practice management |
| **Trustworthy** | Medical data is sacred. We treat it that way |

### Brand Voice

- Clear, confident, never jargon-heavy
- Friendly but not frivolous
- Smart but not condescending
- Helpful but not intrusive

**Example:**
- Not: "Utilize our appointment scheduling functionality"
- Yes: "Schedule smarter. Your time matters."

## II. Logo Concepts

### The Challenge

The old Dentify logo was built around "D + Fingerprint + Tooth" -- three meanings in one symbol. Brilliant for "Dentify" but completely tied to that name.

For Orisios, we need something that:
1. References "os/oris" (mouth) in a sophisticated way
2. Works as both full logo and app icon
3. Feels modern, tech, and trustworthy
4. Is not another generic tooth icon

---

### Concept 1: "The Smile Arc" (Recommended)

**Philosophy:** The letter "O" from Orisios transforms into a stylized smile arc -- the universal symbol of oral health outcome. It's not a tooth (clinical), it's the result of good dental care (emotional).

```
Visual Description:

       ╭──────────╮
      ╱            ╲
     │   ╭──────╮   │     The outer O contains
     │   ╰──────╯   │     an inner smile arc
      ╲            ╱
       ╰──────────╯

Full logo: [O symbol] risios
App icon:  Just the O with inner arc
```

**Key Elements:**
- Outer circle: represents completeness, wholeness, the "O"
- Inner arc: the smile, the goal, the human outcome
- Negative space creates depth without complexity
- Can be animated (smile "draws" itself)

**Color Application:**
- Gradient from Deep Teal to Ocean Teal (sophisticated, medical)
- Coral accent on the inner smile arc (warmth, humanity)

**Why this works:**
- Instant recognition of "smile" = dental
- Letter "O" reinforced = Orisios
- Simple enough for 16x16 favicon
- Distinctive among competitors

---

### Concept 2: "The Oral Gateway"

**Philosophy:** The mouth is a gateway -- to health, to communication, to diagnosis. Two curved lines suggesting an opening, an entrance, a portal.

```
Visual Description:

        ╭─╮   ╭─╮
       ╱   ╲ ╱   ╲
      │     ╳     │      Two curves meeting
       ╲   ╱ ╲   ╱       like lips parting
        ╰─╯   ╰─╯

Or simplified:

        ╭─────╮
       ╱       ╲          Upper curve
      ╱         ╲
       ╲       ╱          Lower curve
        ╰─────╯
```

**Key Elements:**
- Two organic curves suggesting lips/mouth opening
- Symmetry conveys balance and professionalism
- Abstract enough to avoid being literal
- Works in solid color or gradient

**Color Application:**
- Teal outer curve (technology, trust)
- Coral inner curve (care, warmth)

**Why this works:**
- Direct reference to "oris" (mouth) without being clinical
- Elegant, almost architectural
- Feels premium, European
- Scalable

---

### Concept 3: "The Orbis" (Circle of Care)

**Philosophy:** "Orbis" (Latin: circle) combined with "oris" (mouth). A continuous circle representing the cycle of care -- prevention, treatment, maintenance, health.

```
Visual Description:

      ╭──────────────╮
     ╱    ╭──────╮    ╲
    │    ╱        ╲    │
    │   │    ●    │    │     Concentric circles
    │    ╲        ╱    │     with focal point
     ╲    ╰──────╯    ╱
      ╰──────────────╯

App icon simplified:

        ╭─────╮
       ╱   ●   ╲
      │         │
       ╲       ╱
        ╰─────╯
```

**Key Elements:**
- Concentric circles = continuous care cycle
- Central point = the patient (focal point of everything)
- Circular motion = completeness, no gaps in care
- Can be animated (gentle rotation or pulse)

**Color Application:**
- Outer ring: Deep Teal
- Inner ring: Light Teal
- Center point: Coral

**Why this works:**
- Philosophy matches product (complete practice management)
- Geometric, tech-forward
- Unique in dental space
- Works beautifully in dark mode

## IV. Typography

### Primary: Inter

**Why Inter:**
- Designed specifically for screens
- Excellent readability at all sizes
- Modern, neutral, professional
- Free (Google Fonts)
- Used by Figma, Linear, Vercel -- best-in-class company

**Font Stack:**
```css
--font-primary: 'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;
```

**Why Satoshi:**
- Geometric with subtle warmth
- Works beautifully for headings
- Pairs perfectly with Inter
- Free (Fontshare)
- Gives Orisios that extra "premium" edge

```css
--font-display: 'Satoshi', 'Inter', sans-serif;
```

## V. Visual Language

### Design Principles

#### 1. "Clinical Warmth"
We're medical software, but we're not cold. Every interaction should feel professional yet human. Think Apple Store, not hospital waiting room.

#### 2. "Intelligent Simplicity"
We have powerful features (AI, analytics, charts) but we present them simply. Complexity on the backend, clarity on the frontend.

#### 3. "Living Interface"
Orisios feels alive -- subtle animations, real-time updates, responsive feedback. Not static screens, but a breathing organism.

#### 4. "Trustworthy by Default"
Every design choice should reinforce trust. Consistent patterns, predictable behavior, secure feeling.

### Spacing System (8px Grid)

```css
--space-1: 4px;   /* Tight */
--space-2: 8px;   /* Base unit */
--space-3: 12px;  /* Comfortable */
--space-4: 16px;  /* Standard gap */
--space-5: 24px;  /* Section spacing */
--space-6: 32px;  /* Large gap */
--space-7: 48px;  /* Section break */
--space-8: 64px;  /* Page sections */
```

### Border Radius

```css
--radius-sm: 4px;   /* Small elements, badges */
--radius-md: 8px;   /* Cards, buttons, inputs */
--radius-lg: 12px;  /* Modals, large cards */
--radius-xl: 16px;  /* Feature cards, hero elements */
```

**Custom Icons Needed:**

### Motion Design

```css

--ease-out: cubic-bezier(0, 0, 0.2, 1);
```

**Animation Guidelines:**
- Use motion purposefully, never decoratively
- Micro-interactions: 150-200ms
- Page transitions: 300-500ms
- Loading states: subtle, not distracting

## VI. Logo Versions & Usage

### Primary Versions

1. **Full Logo (Horizontal)**
   - Symbol + "Orisios" wordmark
   - Use: Headers, marketing, documents
   - Min width: 120px

2. **Stacked Logo**
   - Symbol above wordmark
   - Use: Square formats, social profiles
   - Min width: 80px

3. **Symbol Only**
   - Just the O/smile mark
   - Use: App icon, favicon, small spaces
   - Min size: 24px

4. **Wordmark Only**
   - Just "Orisios" in brand type
   - Use: When symbol is nearby
   - Min width: 80px

### Color Variations

| Version | Background | Symbol | Text |
|---------|------------|--------|------|
| **Primary** | White/Light | Oris Teal | Night |
| **Inverted** | Oris Teal | White | White |
| **Dark Mode** | Night | Ocean Teal | White |
| **Monotone** | Any | Night | Night |

### Clear Space

Minimum clear space around logo = height of the "O" symbol on all sides.

### Don'ts

- Don't rotate the logo
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, bevels)
- Don't change the colors arbitrarily
- Don't stretch or compress
- Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast
- Don't recreate in different fonts

## VIII. OrisAI Visual Identity

The AI assistant deserves its own visual treatment within the Orisios brand.

### OrisAI Avatar

**Concept:** A friendly, abstract representation combining:
- The Orisios symbol (O with smile)
- Subtle "AI" suggestion (neural network dots, gradient shimmer)

**Color:** Oris Teal with Coral highlights for "speaking" states

### Chat Interface Colors

```css
/* OrisAI messages */
--orisai-bg: #E8F6F6;
--orisai-text: #1A1A2E;
--orisai-accent: #0D7377;

/* User messages */
--user-bg: #FF6B6B;
--user-text: #FFFFFF;
```

### Animation States

- **Idle:** Gentle "breathing" pulse
- **Thinking:** Subtle shimmer effect
- **Speaking:** Waveform or dots animation
- **Complete:** Brief Coral highlight flash

## X. Summary

Orisios represents an evolution from Dentify -- not just a name change, but a maturation of the brand. The new identity:

1. **Embraces the etymology** -- "os/oris" directly connects to oral health
2. **Maintains visual continuity** -- Deep Teal + Coral palette remains
3. **Elevates the sophistication** -- Typography and design language refined
4. **Creates room for AI** -- OrisAI has space to shine within the brand

The "Smile Arc" logo concept is my strong recommendation. It's simple, memorable, meaningful, and will serve Orisios well for years to come.
